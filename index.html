K.D. REGLA

K.D. Regla (she/her) is a New Yorker, born and raised. Of Dominican and Puertorican ancestry, she became inspired to write about the incredible stories of survival she had come across, while living in the concrete jungle. She especially aims to focus on bilingual stories, incorporating the unofficial language of Latinx NYC, “Spanglish”.

K.D. currently lives in upstate New York with her husband and fur babies.
